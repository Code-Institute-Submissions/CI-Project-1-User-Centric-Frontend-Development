My story so far...

1996

Ukraine

I was born in 1996, in small city on the west of Ukraine. As a young boy I was very ordinary, with good friends in shcool, I was a bit of a trouble maker, but I got good results in school so my mam was happy.

2005

Ireland

As a nine year old I moved to Ireland with no english and knowing nothing about what the future would hold. I was very lucky to make some Irish friends, and within 6 months I was speaking english, to this day I feel very blessed to have moved to Ireland. I feel greateful to my parents for taking the risk, as it has brought me many opportunities I other wise would of not had.

2015

Trinity College

Since coming to Ireland, I have always dreamed of studying at Trinity College. In 2015 I sat my leaving certificate, I did very well in my exams, recieving 540/625 points and getting my second course choice of BESS at Trinity College. I was very proud of myself for getting what I worked so hard for. To this day it is one of my greatest achievements, as only a few years prior to this, I didnt know english.

2017

South East Asia

In 2017, after droping out of BESS, I went solo back packing around south east asia for 7 months. I kept studying online on Coursera and Udemy as well as strarted my first real business, which sustained me while traveling. I left BESS, as I needed to find myself and also felt that what I was studying online at the time brought me more value. I however wanted to go back to study in Trinity once more, this time as a more mature person.

2018

Elkstone, Trinity College x2

in 2018 after returning from my solo traveling trip, I received an offer in a very prestigous family office in Dublin, where I worked until march of 2020. During this time I also went back to Trinity College to do a post graduate certificate in Entrepreneurship and Innovation. Business is where my passion lies and I was happy to do this.

2020

Google, Trinity College x3

I tried to make the most of 2020, in late january I entered a cometition in Google at the tech start up weekend, where over the course of the weekend we had to build a business. Our idea got the second prize, since then I have been working on this idea with my two co-founders, I also enroled into a full stack software development course with the code institute. I also got into study a Masters in Management at Trinty College which I postponed until September 2021 after loosing my job.
